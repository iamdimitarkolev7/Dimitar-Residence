Villa Samuilovo

5 BEDROOMS

INTERIOR SPACE: 854 M2

A country house in Krushare, where the force of history combines with the beauty of nature.

Surrounded by nature you can spend some fun days with friends and relatives discovering Krushare's countryside.

The restaurant next to this villa has a great cuisine offering typical tasty bulgarian meals such as banitsa, tarator, etc.

The souvenir shops near the villa offer hand-made souvenirs which are unique in the world. Here you can find 3D-model of the first bulgarian plane and also, you can buy an amazing paintings which are created by the best painer in Bulgaria
